    What we do?

    Awareness

    The club handles various mental health concerns faced by students as well as holds plenty of awareness programs on aspects of Mental Health.

    Open Space

    It aims to promote a safe, non-judgemental and healthy space for university students to share their concerns and address them in a systematic manner.

    Activities

    We regularly conduct events that help students in a holistic manner, including group counsellings and seminars, as well as fun and interesting workshops.

    Open for All

    Open to students from any department and year of study from the university.

    Healthy Competition

    Our club will also be conducting competitions to foster a healthy competitive attitude among the students.

    Open for Collaborations

    We are open to collaborating with different other clubs in the university to conduct joint workshops and events. This promotes a sense of community and support for the students.
