Ground Rules :


  • Respect each and every person in the group their privacy.
  • Strict No to Judgement, Criticism, unhealthy debates.
  • Be active available and open to help and be helped.
  • No usage of phones during the events.
  • Should not speak when another person is speaking.
  • Do not interpret a Topic in between.
  • Feel free to ask questions without fear and hesitation.
  • Respect each and every Club member and the Facilitators.
  • Cooperate and Coordinate when it comes to working with coordinators.
  • Maintain strict Privacy and confidentiality that whatever we discuss or talk in the club should be in the club and never ever discuss outside as we must respect everyone's privacy.
  • Should not bother any member in the group using their phone numbers or any details shared during events.
  • Avoid using abusive languages and gestures which cause disturbance in group.
  • Stick to the aspects of mental health and don't bring irrelavant things or posts into the group or events.
  • The club is free of caste, region, language, race and all related barriers.
  • Be supportive to each other.

Rules and Regulations :


  • Be active in the group and involve in proactive participation.
  • Attendance will be taken.
  • It is mandatory to attend 80% of the events out of 100% of events.
  • Consecutive missing of 3 events leads to removal from the group.
  • Enjoy every moment with open heart and mind without making others uncomfortable
